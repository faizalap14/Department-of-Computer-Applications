2017-2018
Under the auspices of Computer Applications and IT Association, Department of Computer Applications conducted a Special Programme on 23 March 2018 at 4 p.m. at Sir Syed Ahmed Khan Convention Hall in our College. Prof. A. Srinivasan, Asst. Professor of Computer Science and Applications, KMG College of Arts and Science, Gudiyattam was the Chief Guest and delivered a talk on “Cloud Computing and its Technologies” Prof. P.Rizwan Ahmed, Head of the Department of Computer Applications welcomed the gathering. Dr. P. M. Aadil Ahmed Principal delivered the presidential Address.

2019-2020
The Department of Computer Applications and PG Department of Information Technology organized an Association Function on 27th January 2020 at 3.00pm. Prof. S.Abirmai, Head & Asst. Professor and Prof.J.Sasirekha, Asst. Professor of Computer Applications, Marudhar Kesari Jain College for Women, Vaniyambadi delivered an enthusiastic speech on the topic “ Internet of Things”. Dr.M.Mohamad Yunus Principal presided over the function and delivered motivating lecture. Dr.P.Rizwan Ahmed, Head, Department of Computer Applications and PG Department of Information Technology welcomed the gathering.

Certificate Course in Web Designing (CCWD)


The course was conducted by the Department of Computer Science and Department of Computer Application. Our Secretary Sahib inaugurated this course on 9 September 2019. Prof. S. Joseph Gabriel and Dr. P. Rizwan Ahmed were the resource persons of the course. 120 candidates (70 students from shift I and 50 students from shift II) joined this course and also attended the classes regularly. A book consisting of all relevant material for the course was prepared by the Department of Computer Science of our College. The book was also launched by our Secretary and Correspondent Alijanab Alhaj M. Nazar Mohamed Sahib at the function. The valedictory function for this course was conducted on 1 November 2019. Dr. P. Rizwan Ahmed, Prof. Incharge, Shift II welcomed the gatherings. Executive Secretary and former Principal Dr. P. M. Aadil Ahmed delivered the valedictory address. In his address he emphasized our student to equip with more skills to face the challenges of the worlds. He distributed the certificates to the successful students of the course. Dr. M. Mohamed Yunus, Principal i/c and Dr. A. Shafee Ahmed Khan, Prof. Incharge Shift I delivered the felicitation address.

2020-2021

One Day Workshop on Artificial Intelligence Bots


The Department of Computer Science and Computer Applications organized an One Day Virtual Workshop on Artificial Intelligence Bots on 21sh May 2021 at 10.00 am. Prof. U. Sinthuja, Asst. Professor, Department of Information Technology, Hindustan College of Arts and Science, Coimbatore delivered a lecture on the topic “Artificial Intelligence Bots”. Prof.S.Joseph Gabriel, Head, Department of Computer Science welcomed the gathering. Dr.K.H.Kaleemullah Principal i/c presided over the function. Prof.U.Sinthuja, enriched the gathering with the insightful understanding about the concepts of Artificial Intelligence Bots. Dr.P.Rizwan Ahmed, Head, Department of Computer Applications and PG Dept. of Information Technology proposed the Vote of Thanks. Prof.S.Joseph Gabriel and Dr.P.Rizwan Ahmed were the Convenor of this Workshop.

Virtual Short Term Course on Machine Learning for Data Science using Python


The Department of Computer Science & Computer Application organized Six Days Online Short Term Course on Machine Learning for Data Science using Python from 18/05/2021 to24/05/2021. The course was designed for our college students for free of cost. The timing was from 4:30 pm to 5.30 pm conducted through zoom app. Around 65 students participated in the course. Prof.S.Joseph Gabriel and Dr.P.Rizwan Ahmed were the Convenor of this Workshop.
