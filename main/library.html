Welcome to the Central Library, which is one of the central support services of the college. The Central library is well equipped with modern facilities and resources in the form of Books, Journals, Magazines, Dailies, CD-ROM/DVD, On-line databases, E-Journals, E-Books, Audio Video Cassettes, Books, Journals, Project reports etc…

Library, the gate way to knowledge has ample collection of around 48,676 Books, and 84 Periodicals (ie., Journals and Magazines ) to enrich and empower knowledge to the user community. The library runs on MODERNLIB (Automation software) Software for LMS ie., Library Management System

    Rules

  1. Perfect silence should be observed in the Library.

  2. Both U.G as well as PG Students are allowed to borrow two books for a period of one week with one renewal. For M.Phil. /Ph.D Scholars three books are permitted with one renewal. Staff members are allowed to borrow fifteen books for a period of one semester.

  3. Students can avail transaction facilities between 8 a.m. and 4.00 p.m. on all working days

  4. Books should be returned on or before the due date, which is marked on the first page of the books issued.

  5. A fine of Re.1 will be imposed per day for late returning of books.

  6. Books will be issued only on production of barcoded Identity card.

  7. Loss of Identity card should be brought to the notice of the Librarian in writing immediately.

  8. Library will be closed on Sundays and all Government Holidays.

  9. Reference Books, Magazines, Journals and Dailies cannot be taken out of the Library and should not be removed from their places.

  10. No member is allowed to pass on the Library books to anyone, who is neither a student nor a teacher of our College, inside or outside the College. They will do so under pain of fine or of suspension of membership of the Library.

  11. Students or Staff who leave the college for any reason in the middle of a term should return the books before leaving the College.

  12. Writing or underlining in a book or tampering with a book in any manner is strictly prohibited.
