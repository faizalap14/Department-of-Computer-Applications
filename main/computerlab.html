LAB CODE OF CONDUCT

    Do’s

  1. Students must use the computer lab for academic related activities

  2. Students must inform the incharge Lecturer of any observed hardware or software failures

  3. Students must keep their work area clean and orderly.

  4. Students must properly reference all material found on the Internet.

  5. Students must regularly clean their systems by cleaning unnecessary data.

    Don’ts

  1. Students must not disrupt, or trouble the other students in the lab.

  2. Students must not interfere or tamper anyone else’s work or computer.

  3. Students must not access any computer or data without permission.

  4. Students must notuse pen drives to avoid transfer of virus.

  5. Students must not use the computers for games, email, chat rooms or shopping.

  6. Students must not plagiarize material from the Internet or other resources.

  7. Students must not use or copy any unauthorized software or data or submit other academic work for their projects.

  8. Students must not have food or eatables inside the lab.

  9. Students must not access inappropriate material from the Internet.
