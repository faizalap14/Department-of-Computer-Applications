Department of Computer Applications

The Department of Computer Applications was established in 2001. Computer Application is a fast-moving field that includes the disciplines of Mathematics and Programming. We train students to keep pace with the changes in the developments in all the fields of Computer Technology. Our students develop practical skills such as programming oriented (Java, Python, Oracle, PHP,), visual basic.net packages, graphics and hardware oriented (digital logic and microprocessor). We equip our students to attain special knowledge of computer science theories, methods, practices and strategy and specific understanding of computing architecture and design.
